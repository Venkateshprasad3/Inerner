Tips for Interview:

  • Ask questions
  • The interviewer will purposely give you a vague question in hopes of you asking the necessary questions to clarify it for yourself.
  • Don’t panic. I know it can be nerve racking to go on an interview, especially if it’s your first.
  • But, nerves are only going to make it worse. Collect yourself, and if you don’t know the answer, it’s okay. Think through it for a few minutes and I’m sure anyone can come up with a solution.
  • They are there to help you and guide you through the problem.
  • Other than that, study, study, study. Do as many algorithmically challenging problems as you can
